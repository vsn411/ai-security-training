# RakFort AI Security Policy

## Data Handling
All user data processed by RakFort AI systems must be classified before processing.
Personal data (GDPR Article 4) may only be processed with a lawful basis.
AI systems must not retain personal data beyond the stated retention period.

## AI System Registration
All AI systems deployed by RakFort must be registered in the AI system inventory
before going live. Registration requires: system name, risk classification,
data processed, owner, and a completed threat model.

## Incident Response
Security incidents involving AI systems must be reported within 72 hours
under GDPR Article 33 obligations. The AI Security Incident Response team
can be reached at security@rakfort.com.

## Acceptable Use
RakFort AI assistants may be used for: customer service, internal knowledge retrieval,
code assistance, and document summarisation.
They must not be used for: automated decision-making without human oversight,
processing sensitive personal data without DPA approval,
or any purpose outside their registered use case.

## Password Policy
All RakFort systems require passwords of minimum 16 characters.
Passwords must be rotated every 90 days.
Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all production systems.
